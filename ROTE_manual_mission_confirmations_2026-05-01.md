# ROTE Manual Mission Confirmations

Saved: 2026-05-01

This document captures the manually confirmed mission definitions we reviewed together after comparing the app against the in-game ROTE map/wiki.

Important global rule:
- Neutral characters are allowed in general Dark Side, Light Side, and Mixed combat missions unless the mission instead has a narrower faction/tag restriction or a specific required-unit rule.
